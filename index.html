Xin Liu

A light manipulator

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at Computational Imaging & Mixed Representation Laboratory (WeLight Lab), The University of Hong Kong (HKU).

I received my PhD in Optical Engineering in 2023 from X Lab, State Key Laboratory of Extreme Photonics and Instrumentation, College of Optical Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University (ZJU). During the PhD, I was a Visiting Student Researcher at WeLight Lab in Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at HKU. I received my BS in Optoelectronic Information Science and Engineering in 2018 from School of Physics and Optoelectronics, South China University of Technology (SCUT).

I have a broad interest in Computational Optics, currently with a particular focus on Optical Simulation, Light Shaping, Computational Imaging, Adaptive Optics, Far-field Optical Microscopy (Nanoscopy), and Optical Fabrication & Testing.
